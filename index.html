Approach: Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulation using ANSYS Fluent to model velocity distribution, pressure, and turbulence.

Outcome: Obtained detailed flow patterns and energy distribution to support hydraulic design evaluation.

Tools: ANSYS Fluent

Drainage System (Srikana)

Purpose: To design an effective drainage system to reduce flood risk in Srikana, Surabaya.

Approach: Runoff analysis based on rainfall intensity, catchment area, and channel capacity calculation.

Outcome: Proposed drainage dimensions and layout capable of accommodating peak discharge.

Tools: HEC-RAS, Ms. Excel, Ms. word, Canva

Stilling Basin

Purpose: To design a stilling basin as an energy dissipation structure downstream of Candi Limo weir.

Approach: Hydraulic analysis to determine flow characteristics and energy reduction requirements.

Outcome: Developed a stilling basin design that minimizes erosion and ensures downstream stability.

Tools: ANSYS Fluent, AutoCAD, Ms. Excel, Ms. Word, Geostudio, Sketchup, Canva
